The Technology

Magnetism

Magnetism is a part of the natural environment for all living things. Over time, modern civilization has diminished our contact with the Earth’s magnetic field, and Nikken products with magnetic technology are designed to help counterbalance this effect by replicating the conditions that more closely correspond to nature. RAM™ (radial-axis magnetism) Technology in the Nikken Naturest® Kenkopad and Nikken Naturest® Pillow features spherical neodymium magnets which produce a series of overlapping magnetic fields. This magnetic technology produces a cocoon of magnetism that envelops the sleeper.

Far Infrared

All living organisms are dependent on far-infrared energy wavelengths originating from sunlight. Nikken employs Far-Infrared Technology within the sleep product materials to absorb energy and reflect it.

The release of heat energy is regulated such that people using the Kenko duvets and mattress covers do not get cold during winter, nor overheated during summer. Furthermore partners within the same bed find their respective temperature differences are neutralised. Nikken Far-Infrared Technology absorbs energy from a variety of sources — sunlight, body or room temperature — and releases it as energy in the far-infrared part of the spectrum. Energy-reactive fibres constantly receive intake energy and reflect it as gentle warmth. In warmer weather, these fibres reach a limit in the amount of energy absorbed, and the excess heat will pass through the quilt material and escape.

Negative Ions

Negative ions are charged particles typically found in forests, and near water, and their electrical charge is another energy form. Ionic Comfort Technology is the name of a subsystem within Sleep System components that generates negative ions. These odourless, invisible, charged particles, identical to those found in the air in forests and near waterfalls, are emitted by fibres in the quilt and assist in imparting a sense of relaxation.

Triphase

Each Nikken product incorporates at least one of the above innovations. Look out for the Triphase logo indicating products incorporating all three technologies together.
